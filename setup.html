02  / Check it's working

Make sure you're tunnelled.

Connected? Search "what is my IP".

It should show a Sydney, Australia location, not your home or school. Then open a site that's normally blocked and it should load. If you see Sydney, you're all set.

03  / Troubleshooting

If something's off.

It won't connect / says "received real certificate"
Your antivirus is almost certainly scanning HTTPS and getting in the way. In Bitdefender / Kaspersky / ESET / Avast, turn off Encrypted / SSL web scan, or add the Hiddify (or v2rayN) app to its exceptions. Reconnect. Still stuck? Message us.
Connected, but pages won't load
Toggle the connection off and back on. Make sure your device's clock is set to automatic (a wrong clock breaks the tunnel). On a laptop, try switching Hiddify to Proxy mode in its settings.
It's slow
Disconnect and reconnect first. If it's still slow, message us and we can move you to a fresher server.
It worked, then stopped at school
The school may have blocked that server. Message us and we'll rotate you to a fresh link, usually the same day. Your subscription keeps running, so there's nothing to re-buy.
I lost my link
No worries, message us with the email you ordered under and we'll resend it.
Nothing installs on my school Chromebook
Fully locked-down, school-managed Chromebooks block all app installs, so no VPN can run on them. That's a device restriction, not HyperTunnel. Use your own phone on the school WiFi instead and it works fine.

Still stuck? We'll sort it.
